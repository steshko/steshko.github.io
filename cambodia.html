Geography

Cambodia

  • is located on the Indochineese mainland in Southeast Asia.
  • is bordered by Thiland, Laos, and Vietnam
  • has rivers allowing it to trade with its neighbors
  • also borders the Gulf of Thailand giving it access to sea trade routes
  • Cambodia has high average temperatures, around 27.7 deg Celsius

Government

Type and other info

  • Cambodia, officialy – the Kingdom of Cambodia – is a parliamentary constitutional monarchy.
  • Its capital is located in Phnom Penh and is in UTC + 7 time zone.
  • Cambodia is split up into 24 provinces and 1 municipality – the capital
  • Cambodia uses a civil law system – meaing its laws are enacted legislativly rather than through precedent

Economy

Economic Growth

The largest economic sectors in Cambodia are:

  1. Tourism
  2. Garment
  3. Construction
  4. Realestate
  5. Agriculture

In total Cambodia's economy grows at an average rate of 7% per year since 2011.

Wealth and Poverty

Cambodia is one of the poorest countries in Asia

Although Cambodia's economy is growing it is inhibited by high income inequality, corruption and low human resources

The world bank in 2016 upgraded Cambodias classification to lower middle-income

Sustained growth has helped Cambodia's economy but the majority of people do not see this growth and remain in Poverty

Conscriptions

Legal minimum age for service in the Cambodian millitary is 18

Cambodia does have a mandatory conscription stipulating all men ages 18-23 are obliged to serve for 18 months although this law is loosely enforced. Women may volunteer for millitary service although are not required to do so. However, both men and women are required to register with the state upon reaching 18 years of age for conscription in case of a war.

Women make up around 6% of Cambodian active millitary personnel
